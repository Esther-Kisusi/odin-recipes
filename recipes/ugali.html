Ugali

Home

Description

Ugali can be described as stiff porridge made from maize flour. It's a staple food in several African countries.

Ingredients:

  • Maize flour
  • water

Steps:

  1. Put the water in a pan and add a small amount of maize flour to make a porridge.
  2. Stir the porridge until it thickens and then let it boil for 2 minutes.
  3. Slowly add flour into the porridge while stirring to avoid lumps.
  4. Continue adding flour slowly until the porridge is just thick enough (not too soft like a paste nor too hard to mix).
  5. After the ugali is properly mixed with no lumps, serve! Side dishes can be vegetables, meat etc.
