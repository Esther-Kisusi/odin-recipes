Butternut Soup

Home

Description

Butternut soup is a hearty soup made with butternut and a few other ingredients that is best served hot with a side of toast bread.

It's a very quick and easy recipe that anyone can follow.

Ingredients:

  • 1 butternut
  • 1 onion
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 piece of ginger root
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Chop the butternut, carrot, onion and one piece of ginger root.
  2. Boil them in salt and water until they are soft (make sure enough water remains).
  3. Turn off the heat, add a little pepper then blend the soup with a hand blender or any other blender until it's smooth.
  4. If the soup gets too think while blending, add water.
  5. Reheat it a little bit after blending if it cools down.
  6. Garnish and serve with toast bread.
